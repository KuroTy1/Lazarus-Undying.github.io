About Lazarus

Awaken Lazarus is a luxury streetwear brand dedicated to crafting garments that "give life and cultivate community." Their high-fashion collections include hoodies, tees, and hats, designed to elevate wardrobes and magnify purpose. The brand's mission is to produce life-giving streetwear that builds community, elevates your wardrobe, and magnifies your purpose.

Additionally, Lazarus Clothing is an emerging clothing company based in the Caribbean. They are active on social media platforms such as Instagram and Twitter, where they describe themselves as an "up and coming clothing company."

Streetwear meets comfort! Rock this edgy oversized hoodie and cargo sweatpants set for the ultimate laid-back yet stylish vibe. Perfect for effortless everyday wear!
